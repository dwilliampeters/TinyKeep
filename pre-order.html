Pre-order TinyKeep

TinyKeep is currently under active development, however you can pre-order the game at a discounted price. There is no official release date announced yet, but we expect to finish the game sometime at the end of 2014. All money made from pre-order sales will go towards development of additional features - at the moment we’ve got our eye on online co-operative multiplayer!

  • 1 DRM-free copy of TinyKeep for Windows or Mac
  • Digital copy of the TinyKeep Original Soundtrack by James Cobb
  • Early Beta builds and access to the TinyKeep Testing Forum

Alternatively, if you’d like to take advantage of one of our reward tiers, you can use the PayPal donate button on our Kickstarter page. Just make sure to specify which tier you want!
